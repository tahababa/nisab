01What is Nisab Al Zakat?

Nisab Al Zakat is a free, open resource for Muslims and developers. It provides live Nisab thresholds — the minimum wealth at which Zakat becomes obligatory — calculated from live gold and silver prices, across all four major schools of Islamic jurisprudence, in 17 currencies, updated 6 times every day.

Whether you are checking your own obligation, building an app, or automating a Zakat reminder, this site has what you need — no authentication, no rate limits, no registration.
